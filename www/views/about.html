RESEARCH AND STATISTICS UOR CODE DISCLAIMER

The Kentucky State Police (KSP) assigns the Kentucky Uniform Crime Reporting Codes (UOR Codes) to criminal offenses in the Commonwealth of Kentucky. The KSP official list of UOR Codes by KRS number and descriptor can be obtained at the KSP's website @ www.kentuckystatepolice.org. The AOC assigns and maintains codes that are used within the Court of Justice Case Management System for statistical and reporting purposes. These codes are used to identify local and city ordinances, as well as codes for general administrative purposes. The AOC official list of UOR Codes by KRS number and descriptor can be obtained at the Court of Justice Website @ www.kentuckystatepolice.org.
